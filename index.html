What is Campfyer?

Easy art thumbnailing tool for the art blocked.
As creatives, we often feel lost, beaten, and defeated by art block. Our biggest mistake is to internalize that defeat. Campfyer helps by making creativity easier, generating random shapes to inspire you to do what you know best.
